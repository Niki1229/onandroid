On Android Developer Conference 2013

On Android Developer Conference is a developer oriented conference with sessions and workshops on best practices to all approaches to Android development whether Java or HTML5, or C/C++ based. Conference sessions are held Oct 22 and 23 at Moscone North with separate day long training classes held on Oct 21 and 25.

Whether you are just starting with Android and need a good architectural and Java overview or whether you are are a seasoned pro this is a place to sharpen your professional skills on one of the dominant operating systems of today and tomorrow.
